We deeply integrate cutting-edge large model technology with production sites, providing integrated solutions from visual inspection and intelligent scheduling to enterprise knowledge assistants for automotive parts, consumer electronics and other industries.

Our Story: From Family Manufacturing to Independent Venture

Lanzhi DeepTech was founded by Gu Xingzhou with early support in industrial resources and funding from the Gu Group. As the "second generation" of a traditional manufacturing conglomerate, he has spent years shuttling between factory floors and boardrooms, witnessing firsthand the limitations of manual approaches in quality control and production decision-making.

He believes that the next generation of manufacturers should not rely solely on experience and intuition, but instead have intelligent systems that can learn, simulate, and review. Lanzhi DeepTech was born from this belief — inheriting the family's manufacturing roots while exploring a new technology-driven path as an independent company.
